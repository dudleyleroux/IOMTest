Life in the Isle of Man

Knowledge Tests for Settlement & British Nationality applicants

These tests are based on the Life in the Isle of Man supplement to the official "Life in the United Kingdom" guide. Together they cover the history, government, culture, language, law, and everyday life of the Isle of Man across 100 questions.
